About Us


We are the small carpentry shop with the broad knowledge in wood and fine carpentry. We don't just deliver a product, we give you as a customer peace of mind that what you buy is of high quality, of the right material and manufactured in the right way to last as long as possible.

About the Company:

The company was started in 2000 by Mats Hjorting. A small but large workshop of 200 square meters, and is shared with another self-employed person. Has an extraordinary feeling for wood and craftsmanship.

Specialized in:
  • Doors
  • Windows
  • Tables
  • Wardrobes
  • Shelves

That's how it goes:

You call us with your request. Once we have received your request, we can present you with a quote. Approval of the order then takes place with a signed quotation . Then we can also discuss solutions and details on site. Before manufacturing, we send a partial invoice. Manufacturing takes place in our carpentry in kristinehamn. We deliver and assemble when the carpentry is finished or by the date we have agreed upon. When the project is completed, we send a final invoice.

How to send a request:

Feel free to contact us where you describe your ideas or your project. If possible, attach photos, reference images and preferably a drawing. A simple hand sketch is usually enough for us to familiarize ourselves with the project and give an initial price indication.
